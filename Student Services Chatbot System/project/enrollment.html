Class Registration & Course Enrolment

Step-by-Step Guide to Registering for Classes

Follow the steps below to successfully register for classes for the upcoming semester.

  • Step 1: Review the Course Catalog
    Browse the Course Catalog to identify the courses you want to enroll in. Ensure that you meet the prerequisites for each course.
  • Step 2: Check Important Dates
    Make sure to be aware of the registration opening and closing dates, as well as any deadlines for dropping or adding classes.
  • Step 3: Log in to the Student Portal
    Visit the Student Portal and log in using your Enrollment Number and Password.
  • Step 4: Select Courses
    Navigate to the "Course Registration" section and select the courses you wish to enroll in. Add them to your schedule and ensure there are no time conflicts.
  • Step 5: Confirm Registration
    After adding your desired courses, click "Submit" to finalize your registration. You will receive a confirmation email with your registered courses.
  • Step 6: Pay Tuition Fees
    Visit the Fee Payment section in the portal to pay your tuition fees. Payment is required before the registration deadline to avoid cancellation of your courses.

Features of the Enrollment System

  • Real-time Seat Availability: View how many seats are still available for each course before enrolling.
  • Course Conflict Checker: The system will automatically alert you if two courses have overlapping times.
  • Course Waitlist: If a course is full, you can join the waitlist and be automatically enrolled if a seat opens up.
  • Drop/Add Period: Modify your course selection during the first two weeks of the semester without penalty.
  • Prerequisite Checking: Ensure you meet all the prerequisites before enrolling in advanced courses.
