Health & Wellness Services

Available Health Services

We offer a wide range of health services to support students' physical, mental, and emotional well-being. Below are the services you can access:

  • Medical Consultations: General health consultations and treatment for minor illnesses and injuries.
  • Mental Health Counseling: One-on-one counseling sessions to support emotional and mental well-being.
  • On-Campus Pharmacy: Access to over-the-counter medications and prescription refills.
  • Nutrition and Diet Counseling: Personalized dietary advice and meal planning for healthy living.
  • Immunizations: Vaccinations for various preventable diseases including flu, tetanus, and hepatitis.
  • Sexual Health Services: Advice, education, and treatment for sexual and reproductive health.
  • Fitness & Exercise Programs: Free access to gym facilities and group fitness classes.
  • Workshops & Wellness Events: Participate in wellness workshops and events that promote mental and physical well-being.

Mental Health & Counseling Services

Your mental health matters! Our counseling services are designed to provide a safe and confidential space for students to talk about challenges they may be facing, whether related to academic stress, anxiety, depression, relationships, or any other concern.

  • One-on-One Counseling: Book a private counseling session with a licensed counselor.
  • Group Therapy: Attend group sessions with other students facing similar issues, such as stress management or coping with anxiety.
  • 24/7 Crisis Hotline: Reach out to our emergency crisis line if you need immediate support during a mental health emergency.

Emergency Contacts

  • Mental Health Crisis Hotline: 1-800-123-4567
  • On-Campus Health Center: +123 456 7890 (Open Monday - Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM)

Fitness & Wellness Programs

Stay physically active and mentally fit with our wide range of fitness and wellness programs. All students have free access to the on-campus gym and can participate in organized fitness classes.

  • Yoga Classes: Weekly yoga sessions for relaxation and mindfulness.
  • Cardio Workouts: High-energy group classes designed to improve cardiovascular health.
  • Strength Training: Use our fully equipped gym to build muscle and strength.
  • Meditation Workshops: Learn meditation techniques to help reduce stress and improve mental clarity.

Health Insurance & Coverage

We offer comprehensive health insurance plans that cover a range of medical and wellness services. Whether you're seeking medical treatment, counseling, or preventive care, we have you covered.

  • Medical Expenses: Covers doctor visits, hospitalization, and emergency services.
  • Prescription Drugs: Access to prescription medications with partial or full coverage.
  • Mental Health Care: Coverage for therapy and counseling sessions.

For more details on insurance plans and how to enroll, visit the Health Insurance Portal.
