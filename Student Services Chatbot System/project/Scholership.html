Scholarship and Financial Aid

Available Scholarships

Merit-Based Scholarship

This scholarship is awarded to students with exceptional academic performance. It covers up to 80% of tuition fees.

  • Eligibility: GPA of 3.9 or higher
  • Application Deadline: June 31, 2025
  • Required Documents: Academic transcripts, personal statement, recommendation letter

Need-Based Financial Aid

This financial aid supports students from low-income backgrounds, offering up to 100% tuition fee coverage.

  • Eligibility: Family income below $25,000 annually
  • Application Deadline: April 15, 2025
  • Required Documents: Proof of income, financial statements, academic records
Apply Now

Women in STEM Scholarship

Encouraging female students to pursue degrees in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). This scholarship covers up to 70% of tuition fees.

  • Eligibility: Female students enrolled in STEM programs
  • Application Deadline: May 1, 2025
  • Required Documents: Academic records, personal statement, proof of enrollment in a STEM course
Apply Now

International Student Scholarship

Designed for international students, this scholarship offers up to 60% of tuition fees based on academic performance and leadership skills.

  • Eligibility: International students with GPA 3.5 or higher
  • Application Deadline: June 30, 2025
  • Required Documents: Academic transcripts, proof of citizenship, personal statement
Apply Now

Sports Scholarship

For students excelling in sports, this scholarship covers 50% of tuition fees and offers additional benefits for sports equipment and training.

  • Eligibility: Participation in university sports teams
  • Application Deadline: July 15, 2025
  • Required Documents: Proof of sports achievements, coach recommendation letter
Apply Now

Financial Aid Process

We offer various financial aid options to assist students in covering their educational costs. The application process is as follows:

  • Step 1: Complete the financial aid application form available on the student portal.
  • Step 2: Submit necessary documents such as proof of income, tax returns, and financial statements.
  • Step 3: Receive a decision within 4 weeks of application submission.
  • Step 4: If approved, the financial aid will be applied directly to your tuition balance.
